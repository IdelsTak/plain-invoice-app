# Code of Conduct

## Our standard
Be respectful, constructive, and professional.

## Expected behavior
- Assume good intent
- Give specific technical feedback
- Focus criticism on code and decisions, not people
- Keep discussions solution-oriented

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Abusive or hostile language
- Repeated bad-faith disruption of collaboration

## Enforcement
Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content and contributions that violate this code.
Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from project participation.

## Reporting
Report violations to project maintainers through repository issues labeled `conduct` or direct maintainer contact when needed.
